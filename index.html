GNT Inc.'s patented nanocellulose slurry is the cornerstone of our business.

GreenNano Technologies Inc. was established in 2015 as a spin-off company based on University of Toronto sponsored IP. We are located in southern Ontario, Canada.

Our translucent nanocellulose composite films achieve up to 96% transparency.

GNT Inc. produces nanocellulose and nanocellulose-based materials for a wide range of applications, such as:

  • flexible displays and OLEDs

  • high-strength next-generation paper

  • packaging products

  • functional paints and coatings

All of our products are made using patented processes and energy-saving clean technology. See our Products and Applications pages for more details.
