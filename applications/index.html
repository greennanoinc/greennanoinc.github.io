Applications

Nanocellulose Composite Film

Our films are durable, flexible, colorless, transparent, and thermally resistant. They are an ideal substrate for display systems such as OLED, and suitable for the packaging industry due to their exceptional barrier properties.

We have three different variants of NanoFilm:

  • GNT-NanoFilm: regular translucent flexible film

  • GNT-Transparent NanoFilm: thermally stable (below 150°C), transparent (89% @600nm), flexible substrate

  • GNT-Transparent NanoFilm HT190: thermally stable (up to 190°C), transparent (96% @600nm), flexible substrate

OLED/Thermal Laminating Film

Thanks to a recent technological breakthrough, we have developed a highly transparent, colorless, and flexible nanocellulose film with exceptional barrier, optical, and thermal properties.

A number of potential companies, both local and foreign, have expressed interest in developing joint ventures with GNT substrate products.

Packaging

Our product can be used to create food packaging and paper/paperboard products. These products can be enhanced with GNT to:

  • reduce fiber-load (gsm) and energy consumption

  • improve sheet strength and density

  • enhance texture and porosity

  • improve printability

Coatings and Paints

Our proprietary plant protein formulation can be used as a coating in two ways: as a coating on leather upholstery in the automotive industry, and as a coating additive for non-woven substrates in the textile industry.

Due to its extremely high surface area, GNT nanocellulose has also been used in the paint and coatings industry to improve paint durability and achieve greater cover area.

Above is a short demonstration of primer-less paint made with GNT substrate.

Other Applications

Other applications include:

  • Oil drilling: as a drilling mud, which is a viscous fluid mixture used in oil and gas drilling operations to lubricate and cool the drill bit and stabilize the wellbore.

  • Cosmetics: as a moisturizer, non-sticking, rheology thickener, dispersion and thixotropic additive.

Our customers are members of the pulp and paper, food, and automotive industries.
